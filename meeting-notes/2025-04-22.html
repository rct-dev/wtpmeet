Meeting Notes – April 22, 2025

Attendance: 5

Date: Tuesday, April 22, 2025

Ground Rules for Discussion

  • Always show respect for others
  • No personal attacks — challenge ideas, not individuals
  • Avoid extreme labels (e.g., "communist," "nazi," "traitor")
  • Critique upward — public officials and ideas, not each other
  • Context matters — listen for the whole story, not just the soundbite
  • Use video or voice when possible for better human connection

Shared Fears

  • Fear of punishment for personal beliefs
  • Loss of freedom of speech
  • Lack of due process
    • Concerns from Democrats: deportation ("U.S. Citizens next?")
    • Concerns from Republicans/Independents: January 6 prosecutions
  • Fear of politically motivated IRS audits
  • Fear of world organizations threatening U.S. sovereignty and rights
  • Fear of national bankruptcy due to uncontrolled debt

Shared Hopes

  • Government spending and national debt brought under control
  • True freedom of speech protected and honored
  • Legal system that protects all individuals equally, regardless of politics
  • Stronger checks on government power
  • A system that values people over political parties
  • Reduced child hunger and lower food prices
  • Affordable housing available for all
  • Abundant opportunities for good jobs
  • A smaller, less intrusive government
  • Local sovereignty and protected due process rights

Upcoming Discussion Topics

  • Tariffs
  • Deportation
  • Elections
  • Defendant Rights
